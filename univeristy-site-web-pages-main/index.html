Our clubs

We're always looking for new members who are committed to excellence.

OTCE

orange Tech Club aims to train students and support them in preparing for professional life in a context that is becoming increasingly digital. It also aims to detect the entrepreneurial fiber, refine it and structure it through coaching sessions, project management training, and soft skills workshops .

ACM

The ACM Computer Club aims to develop knowledge in advanced algorithms through solid training. all these members have the chance to participate in the international ACM-ICPC competition.

ENISO TEAM

Our goal is to unite students and learn together as a team everything related to mechanics, electronics and programming, which allows us to create our own robots, whether they are autonomous, remote-controlled robots , humanoids or robotic arms, we have left no stone unturned.

IEEE ENISO

The Student Branch’s goal is the dissemination of the knowledge of the theory and practice of all aspects of electrical engineering, computer science and the related arts and sciences, as well as the furtherance of the professional development of the students.

EUREKA

Having the cultural and artistic for status, Eureka shapes and refines the profile of the future engineer thanks to the multidisciplinary aspect of its committees ranging from scientific , to artistic cultural, including humanitarian, ecology, festivities and sporting activities.

AERO

the Aerotronix club aims to promote aeromodelling in the minds of engineering students, a discipline which mixes mechanics, electronics and IT and can be accessible to beginners. by offering a challenge for connoisseurs . Our projects therefore mainly focus on the design and production of RC models
